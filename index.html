Quality engineering as a craft

I'm a Senior SDET and Automation Architect with nearly a decade of experience owning end-to-end quality strategy for enterprise-grade fintech, banking, and healthcare platforms. My work spans from the earliest lines of a sprint through production monitoring — because quality is a shared responsibility, not a gate at the end.

At BNY (via Wipro), I architected a scalable Java + Selenium + TestNG framework now adopted across multiple product teams — compressing a 4-hour regression suite to 45 minutes and leading Playwright adoption for modern UI testing. Beyond the frameworks, I mentor junior engineers — growing team automation coverage by 35% in 6 months at my current role. I work closely with developers and product managers because the best bugs are the ones never written.
